About RallyGDP

RallyGDP is a professional Time-Speed-Distance (TSD) Rally Navigation platform developed for rally drivers and navigators. It combines RoadBook, GPX Navigation, Speed Charts, GPS Tracking, Odometer Correction and Penalty Calculation into one powerful application.
